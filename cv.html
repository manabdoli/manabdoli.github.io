Summary

Mansour Abdoli is a lifelong learner who collaborates with colleagues to address challenges in education and research. He enjoys mentoring future professionals and teaching students of all backgrounds and abilities.

He is currently a full‑time instructor and course coordinator at CSUF, teaching probability and statistics. His multidisciplinary background in electrical engineering, industrial engineering, and statistics supports his work in teaching, research, and engineering practice. He is also experienced in educational technology and has developed a wide range of instructional materials across multiple learning platforms.
